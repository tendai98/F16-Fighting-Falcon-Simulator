# High-G Lesson and Dogfight Training Update

## Removed high-G sensory effects
- Removed high-G / G-limit heartbeat and breathing-style audio loops.
- Removed G-LOC audio muffling.
- Removed visual tunnel-vision, blur and blackout overlays.
- G feedback is now HUD/handling only: `HIGH G`, `G LIMIT`, energy bleed and the FLCS limiter.

## Updated high-G lesson
- Reworked the high-G lesson into target tracking against a docile air target.
- The lesson now teaches G limits in a realistic tracking context instead of isolated free-flight pulls.
- Steps teach tracking, pulling to 5G, finding the G limit, unloading to regain energy, and applying the concept to a short gun track.

## New dogfight / gun defence lesson
- Added a new Flight School lesson: `DOGFIGHT — Guns & Defence`.
- Player is gun-only; A-A missile launches are blocked in this lesson.
- Training bandit is medium-hard and can fire red air-to-air missiles.
- Lesson teaches padlock/lock, missile defence with hard turns, weaves and countermeasures, then re-attack for a gun solution.
